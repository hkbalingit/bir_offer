What abatement covers

✓  Penalties that may be reduced or waived

25% surcharge on tax due 12% per annum delinquency interest Compromise penalties ₱200–₱50,000 Prior unfiled returns (qualifying circumstances) Open BIR cases under amnesty window

ℹ  Basic tax due is not abatable — only surcharges, interest, and compromise penalties may be cancelled. Abatement is approved by the Commissioner of Internal Revenue.

What RMC 47-2026 gives you

  • ✓Penalties for non-filing stop upon submission of complete closure requirements
  • ✓Registration tagged "deregistered" — no more open cases generated
  • ✓Standardized document list — no catch-all requirements
  • ✓File manually at RDO or electronically
  • ✓Micro taxpayers: no mandatory audit upon closure
  • ✓Micro taxpayers (≤₱3M sales / ≤₱8M assets): expedited tax clearance*

⚠ Note on processing timelinesThe 3-working-day clearance is RMC 47-2026's target — not a guaranteed turnaround. Actual time depends on your RDO, completeness of documents, and application volume.
